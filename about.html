About Ivy Edge Strategies

Invite-only business and marketing consultancy

Ivy Edge Strategies was founded in 2024 to serve a small set of leaders who value precision, discretion and results. We operate by referral. Each engagement is tailored to the client’s objectives with a clear plan and measurable outcomes.

Ivy Edge Strategies is a sole proprietorship. The founder holds a BSBA from Boston University Questrom School of Business.

What we do

We help clients sharpen direction, align execution and scale with discipline. Our work spans business strategy and advisory, marketing and positioning, AI integration and Vibe Coding, leadership support.

How we work

  • Clarity first – define the objective, pressure test assumptions, set constraints.
  • Simple plans – focus on the few moves that change outcomes.
  • Execution accountability – timelines, owners, metrics that tie to value.
  • Confidentiality – private work with no social media noise.

Is there a fit

We are selective. If you were referred, mention it. If not, introduce your firm and the problem you want solved. If there is alignment we will respond.
